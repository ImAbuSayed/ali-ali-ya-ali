The echo of the desert wind carries a name,
A melody of grief played on a hollow reed.
With every heartbeat, a heavy strike upon the chest,
The world laments the Lion of God.
Ali, Ali, Ya Ali.
The gates of knowledge open through his blessed soul,
The warrior of light who stood when shadows fell.
His courage turned the tides of destiny and time,
A king who slept on dust, a heart of pure sublime.
He held the sword of justice with a steady hand,
The bravest spirit ever to walk upon the land.
Ali, Ali, Ya Ali, the light of truth remains,
Your wisdom flows like water through our weary veins.
The master of the faithful, the pillar of the path,
Your name is our protection against the worldly wrath.
Ali, Ali, Ya Ali.
The one who broke the idols and the walls of pride,
